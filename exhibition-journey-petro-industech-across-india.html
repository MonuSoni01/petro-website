Our Exhibition And Meet Journey Across India

Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd. has proudly taken part in major trade exhibitions across India. These events have helped us grow our dealer network, introduce new bathroom accessories and hardware products, and expand our Channel Partner Program nationwide.

At each exhibition, we did more than show our products:

  • Live Demos: We demonstrated how our bathroom accessories and hardware products work, and how they can improve any space.
  • Talking to Visitors: We spoke with attendees, answered their questions, and got valuable feedback.
  • New Products: We introduced fresh designs for bathroom accessories and new hardware products.
  • Making Connections: We met with many dealers and distributors, building long-term business relationships.
  • Exclusive Deals: We offered special deals to new and existing partners who visited our exhibitions.

These efforts helped us expand into new regions, form new partnerships, and stay focused on providing high-quality bathroom accessories and hardware products across India.

From North India to South India and North East India, our exhibition journey reflects our commitment to growth, partnership, and quality.
