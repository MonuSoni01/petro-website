Hiring Process

Easy-to-Follow and Effective Hiring Flow

We have simplified our hiring process modalities in such a way that it makes sense to both sides a candidate and a recruiter - as it is easy to understand and execute.

1

Apply

Please use our career form to send your application details and CV relevant to the desired role at Petro Industech.

2

Screening

Our Human Resources (HR) team reviews your application profile and approves you as a potential candidate for the vacancy you requested for.

3

Interview

The shortlisted candidate shows his personality abilities and skills through his freedom to express himself during the interview process.

4

Selection

Based on skills, attitude, and role fitment, the final decision is made for the particular candidate.

5

Onboarding

Some selected candidates start the journey with the team, which involves completing the necessary documentation and training pertinent to the role.
