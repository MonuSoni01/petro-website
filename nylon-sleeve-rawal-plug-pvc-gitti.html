Learning About
Nylon Sleeves and Wall Plugs

How a Nylon Sleeve Works

The nylon sleeve was designed to expand within the wall after the screws are inserted. The expansion holds the screw tightly in place, which stops any movement or loosening. This is among the best solutions for fixing bathroom fittings, kitchen fixtures, electrical boards, signs, frames, furniture, brackets, and numerous other types of installations.

Different Names Across India

In India, technicians and customers use different names for the same product: nylon sleeves, rawal plugs, PVC gitti, PVC wall plugs, and wall plugs. Other names include PVC anchors, PVC fasteners, PVC dobli, and PVC gulli. The name doesn't matter; its function remains identical: secure, solid, long-term, and stable wall support.

Great for Bathroom, Kitchen & Home Installations

Nylon sleeves play an essential role in installing bathroom accessories. Things like towel rings, shelves, towel rods, shampoo holders, soap dispensers, and even front shelves need a sturdy, solid fix. An excellent wall connector will ensure that the items remain securely in place even during daytime use.

A similar level of functionality is expected for kitchens, living spaces, bedrooms, and office spaces. Shelf organisers, hooks, shelves, and decorative items such as mirrors, boards, and electrical fittings are dependent on the safety of a grasp. Nylon sleeves can make these items safer, sturdier, and more robust.

Their ease of use and compatibility with all types of wall structures make them among the most commonly used wall-fixing methods across India.

PVC or Nylon: Choosing the Best Material

We provide nylon sleeves and PVC Gitti. Both are ideal for particular applications. Nylon sleeves provide greater expansion and are ideal in bathroom fixtures, wood panels, kitchen shelves, and heavy-to-medium fittings.

PVC gitti, or PVC gulli, is the ideal choice for lighter- to medium-duty installation, commonly employed by carpenters and electricians to make general-purpose fittings.

Each material has its own use cases, so customers can choose based on the product's weight and material type.

Petro Industech manufactures nylon sleeves in a variety of sizes to meet various installation needs. The smaller sleeves can be used with hooks or frames. Medium sleeves are popular for housing fittings, bathrooms, and accessories. The larger sleeves work well for large brackets, shelves, or port systems.

A proper size will ensure that the screw can expand the sleeves properly and give the greatest grip.
