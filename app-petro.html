How to Redeem Your Points

Follow these simple steps to redeem your reward points and receive the amount directly in your bank account:

  • Tap the Points Icon: Click on the Points icon located at the bottom of the app screen.
  • Click on the "Redeem" Button: On the points screen, tap the "Redeem" button to begin the process.
  • Click on the "Edit" Button: Select the "Edit" option to add or update your bank account details.
  • Enter Bank Account Details: Fill in all the required fields accurately:
    • Beneficiary Name
    • Account Number
    • Confirm Account Number
    • IFSC Code
    • Bank Name
  • Click on "Save": After entering your details, click "Save" to store them securely.
  • Click on "Withdrawal Request": Tap the "Withdrawal Request" button to initiate the point redemption process.
  • Receive Amount in Bank: Your redeemed amount will be credited to your bank account within 24 Hours.
