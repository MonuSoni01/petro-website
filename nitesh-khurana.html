About

Nitesh Khurana

Nitesh Khurana, Managing Director of Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd., leads the company in hardware and bathroom accessories manufacturing. Born on 24 April 1984 in Rajasthan, India, he drives innovation, product quality, and sustainable growth for dealers, distributors, and customers nationwide.

Early Life & Background

Early Life and Background

Nitesh Khurana belongs to a family with a business background in the hardware industry, and he was exposed to business very early because his family was also business-focused.

He worked at the family business early on, which was a small hardware store where he could see the functioning of the business and its customer interactions.

His rise to this role combined real market experience with an in-depth understanding of challenges faced by carpenters, dealers, and distributors.

Over time, these observations formed the foundation of his interest in improving product quality and developing more reliable solutions for the hardware and bathroom accessories market in India.

Market Understanding

Understanding the Core Market Problem

Nitesh Khurana observed, while diving into the hardware industry, that there was a common issue. Carpenters frequently faced issues with wall fittings becoming loose or falling after installation. Fixtures, often with the most careful workmanship and wall-load, did not stay in place because, in most cases, poor-quality supporting materials were used.

Many traditional methods, including wooden fixing techniques, were inconsistent and often failed over time. This created frustration among skilled workers and affected the reliability of their work.

This problem became the foundation of a long-term mission — to build a dependable solution for installation and fixing systems.

During his early exposure to the hardware business, Nitesh Khurana observed a repeated issue faced by carpenters and installers.

Small but critical components used in wall fittings often failed after installation. This created frustration, repeated work, and loss of trust for skilled workers, even when their execution was correct.

He realized that the problem was not effort or skill — it was product quality.

Business Journey

Founding Insight & Direction

01
Insight & Direction

Founding Insight & Direction

Rather than viewing this as a market restriction, Nitesh Khurana focused on developing a solution.

He and his team began investigating different raw materials, analyzing product construction, and exploring manufacturing possibilities. Early periods were marked by multiple trials, errors, and study phases.

The process was not simple. Several attempts failed, raw materials were wasted, and progress was slow. But each failure added new understanding about product behavior and manufacturing standards.

02
Product Journey

Product Development Phase

After continuous experimentation, a stable and reliable solution was developed.

The product was tested in real working conditions by carpenters and professionals. The feedback confirmed one thing clearly — the problem had finally been solved in a practical way.

This moment marked the beginning of a new direction in the business journey.

03
Market Expansion

Expanding into the Market

After product validation, the next challenge was market reach.

Nitesh Khurana began working directly with dealers and distributors in different regions. Some accepted the product early, while others tested it before adopting it.

Gradually, trust started building in the market. The product became known for reliability and consistent performance.

04
Distribution Challenge

Solving a Bigger Challenge: Distribution

As demand grew, a new challenge emerged — safe delivery and product protection.

During transportation, products were sometimes damaged, affecting customer experience.

Instead of ignoring the issue, packaging systems were improved. This step helped increase product safety and strengthen dealer confidence in supply consistency.

Network Growth

Dealer & Distributor Network

Consistent product quality and timely deliveries helped the business expand into new markets.

The system brought in qualified and experienced partners primarily for sustained business relationships rather than one-time transactions, which helped establish a solid network of dealers and distributors.

This network served as the foundation for expanding business growth and increasing operational reach across India.

Future Direction

Expanding the Vision

Over time, the focus expanded beyond a single product category.

Based on feedback from dealers and market needs, new product lines were introduced in the bathroom accessories segment.

Each product was developed with the same principle — practical use, reliable quality, and long-term value.

Company Leadership

Nitesh Khurana, Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd.

Under his leadership, the business became Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd. It turned into a manufacturing company for the hardware and bath accessories industry.

The company keeps its eye on product quality, manufacturing reliability, customer service, and business partnerships as well. With continuous progress and operational upgrades, Petro Industech has been increasing its product collection while staying strongly committed to reliability.

Today, the company serves customers and channel partners across multiple regions of India.

Leadership Philosophy

Leadership Philosophy

Nitesh Khurana believes leadership is built through understanding, responsibility, and continuous improvement.

Having worked across different stages of the supply chain, he understands the issues retailers, wholesalers, distributors, and manufacturers face. This practical knowledge helps guide strategic decisions and business direction across the company.

He leans more toward constructing whole systems rather than relying only on individuals. In his view, steady growth is supported by robust procedures, being accountable, and long-term planning.

Mission

The Mission

To build reliable products and systems that help dealers, distributors, and customers grow together.

He believes business success is meaningful only when it creates value for everyone connected to it.

From observing problems in a small hardware shop to building a structured manufacturing company, the journey reflects one consistent belief — real growth comes from solving real problems.
