What is the Vision for Petro?

At Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd., our vision is to become a leading global brand in the bathroom accessories industry, recognized for innovation, quality, and sustainability. Founded in 2003 in New Delhi, India, Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd. has grown into a trusted name, delivering premium bathroom accessories, hardware, and plastic products for over 22 years.

With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, we aim to revolutionize the industry by offering high-quality, stylish, and durable products at competitive prices. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, we continuously enhance our manufacturing capabilities using advanced injection molding technology and strict quality control measures. Our state-of-the-art facilities, spread over 10,000 sq. ft., ensure efficient production, timely delivery, and long-term business relationships with our clients worldwide.

With a dedicated team of 100+ skilled professionals and a streamlined warehousing operation, we guarantee timely delivery, durability, and unmatched product quality, making Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd. a trusted leader in the industry.

Advanced Manufacturing & Uncompromising Quality Standards

At Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd., we take pride in our advanced manufacturing unit, spread across 10,000+ sq. ft., equipped with the latest injection molding technology and automated machinery to ensure precision and efficiency in production. Our facility is designed to maintain a safe, eco-friendly, and high-performance working environment, allowing us to manufacture top-tier bathroom accessories, hardware, and plastic products at scale.

To uphold our commitment to quality, we follow a strict Quality Management System (QMS) that includes:

  • Raw Material Testing– Ensuring density, viscosity, and molecular weight consistency
  • Process Monitoring – Tracking temperature, pressure, and production parameters
  • Visual Inspection – Detecting cracks, bubbles, warping, and surface defects
  • Dimensional Accuracy – Verifying that each product meets precise size and shape requirements
