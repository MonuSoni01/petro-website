Why Choose Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd.?

With over 22 years of excellence, Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Known as Petro Industries) has established itself as a trusted bath products company and a leading manufacturer of bath accessories and hardware products. Our dedication to quality craftsmanship, innovation, and customer satisfaction makes us a preferred choice in the industry.

Unmatched Quality & Craftsmanship

As an ISO 9001:2015-certified company and a recipient of the Most Trusted Brand of India title, Petro Hardware ensures every product meets the highest industry standards.

Innovative & Sustainable Design

Our wide range of bathroom accessories includes soap dishes, toothbrush holders, tumbler holders, corner shelves, door silencers, nylon sleeves and wall plugs, crafted with advanced injection molding technology for durability and style.

Customer-Centric Approach

With a 10,000 sq. ft. manufacturing facility, a skilled team of 100+ professionals, and a rigorous quality control process, we deliver top-notch products that blend functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability.

At Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd., we are not just manufacturing bath accessories and hardware products we are redefining modern home essentials with superior quality and innovation.
