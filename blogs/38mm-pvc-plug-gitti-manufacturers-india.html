What is a 38mm PVC Plug Gitti?

A 38mm PVC plug Gitti is a wall fixing component used to keep screws securely inside walls. When you put a screw into the plug, the plug expands a bit and then makes a strong grip inside the drilled hole. This helps the fittings stay stable and reduces the chance of loose screws.

The 38 mm PVC Gitti is suitable for many standard installation tasks that require a stronger hold than smaller plugs. It is used by plumbers, electricians, carpenters, contractors, hardware shops, dealers, and distributors for everyday fitting work.

Why is a PVC Plug Useful in Wall Fixing

A PVC wall plug kind of helps screws grip tighter in brick, cement, concrete, and other similar wall textures. Without the right plug, screws can get a bit loose later on, particularly when the fitting is used every day. Then you end up with extra repair work and more customer complaints.

For bathroom accessories, the wall plug is really quite important in practice. Towel rods, soap dishes, shelves, towel rings, tumbler holders, mirrors, hooks, and dispensers all need solid wall support; otherwise, everything can wobble. A good PVC plug helps those items stay fixed properly in place.

How to Choose the Right PVC Plug Size

Choosing the right wall plug depends on what kind of wall you have, the screw diameter, the overall fitting weight, and the reason you are mounting. A 38mm PVC wall plug gitti is handy when the installer wants extra depth and steadier holding support. Ensure that the hole drilled is precisely the same size as the plug, and remove any dust before inserting the plug, as this can affect the grip strength.

For best results, the screw should fit tightly inside the plug and expand it properly. This creates a better grip and improves installation life.
