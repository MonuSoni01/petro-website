Wall plugs are anchors inserted into walls to ensure that screws are held securely. They prevent wobbling and ensure long-term stability.

How to Install Wall Plugs Correctly (Expert Tips)

Step-by-Step Installation

  • Mark the drilling point
  • Choose the right drill bit size (same diameter as the wall plug)
  • Drill the hole straight
  • Clean the dust inside the hole
  • Insert the wall plug fully
  • Tighten the screw gradually
  • Mount the bathroom accessory (towel rod, soap dish, holder, etc.)

Common Installation Mistakes to Avoid

  • Using a plug bigger or smaller than required
  • Not cleaning the drilled hole
  • Using low-quality wall plugs
  • Applying too much force while screwing
  • Using an incorrect drill bit size
