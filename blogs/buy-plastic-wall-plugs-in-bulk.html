🔹 How to use a PVC wall plug?

Drill a hole in line with the wall plug size. Put the PVC wall plug into the hole, make sure it fits, no wobble. Then slide the screw into the plug and tighten it with care. When the screw is pulled in, the plug expands inside the wall, and it keeps the screw stable and firmly.

🔹 How to install a plastic wall plug?

To put in a plastic wall plug, start by marking the exact fitting spot. Next, drill the wall carefully, not too fast. Then clean the hole well, and put in the wall plug. After that, position the fitting item, and tighten the screw until the hold feels solid and steady.

🔹 How do you install a wall plug correctly?

Pick the right plug size based on the screw and also the wall type. Drill a straight opening, put the plug all the way in, then turn the screw slowly until it’s snug. Don’t tighten too much, because it could mar the wall or even weaken the plug.

🔹 What are the different types of wall plugs?

Common wall plug varieties include Plastic wall plugs, PVC wall plugs, nylon wall plugs, rawal plugs, PVC gitti, PVC gulli, PVC anchors, and PVC fasteners. Each plug type is chosen depending on how heavy the fitting is, what the wall surface is like, and what the installation requirements.
