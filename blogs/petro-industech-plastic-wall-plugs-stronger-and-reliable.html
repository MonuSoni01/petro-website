What Are Plastic Wall Plugs and Why Do They Matter?

Plastic wall plugs are small cylindrical anchors inserted into drilled holes to hold screws securely inside brick, concrete, or masonry walls. They:

  • Expand evenly inside the wall
  • Create strong friction
  • Prevent slipping
  • Increase screw holding capacity
  • Enable damage-free installation
  • Safely support heavy loads

Without wall plugs, screws would slip out, leading to:

  • Falling accessories
  • Accidents
  • Broken bathroom fittings
  • Long-term wall damage

Wall plugs are essential for:

  • Bathroom shelves
  • Soap Holder Bathroom
  • Mirrors
  • Cabinets
  • Kitchen fixtures
